1. Description

The dataset, provided by NYPD Police department, involves every shooting incident that occurred in NYC from 2006 to 2023. Each incident recorded contains information about time, date, precinct, Borough, victim profile (age, gender, race), perp profile (age, gender, race) and location information whereever available.
